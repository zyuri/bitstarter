One of the major problems in studying textbooks are fill-in-the-blank fields. Students have to fill-in the words themselves in handwriting. Errors are unevitable. Afterwards these textbooks are used to learn the matter, including the mistakes that were introduced.

Dysleximo is a platform where teachers can create content that is easily accessible for Dyslexics, using fill-in-the-blank fields. Students can register different textbooks, fill in the blanks during the course. Doing this they get help to overcome dyslexia. Afterwards they can use this textbook to study. Students are able to grade textbooks and comment on how to improve the textbook.

FAQ

What does dysleximo stand for?
Dysleximo refers to 'dyslexia eximo'.
Eximo (latin) meaning 'to free from'.
Our platform is meant to free students from the consequences of them being dyslexic.
What's a dyslexic?
A dyslexic is a person who has dyslexia.
What's a dyslexia?
Dyslexia is characterized by difficulty in learning to read fluently and with accurate comprehension despite normal intelligence.
